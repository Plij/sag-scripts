EventLogging

Script for logging events to GameAnalytics.
Event ids that are used: 1 = Spray 2 = Police bust 3 = PlayerBust 4 = rexLogin 5 = rexLogout 6 = Watcher Entity of Spray 7 = Watcher Entity on Police Bust 8 = Police information of Bust
